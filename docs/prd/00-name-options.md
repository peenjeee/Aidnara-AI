# PRD 00 - Brand

## Final Name

**Aidnara AI**

## Meaning

**Aidnara AI** combines three ideas:

- **Aid**: help, donation, and social support.
- **Nara**: a human-centered name feel, suggesting people, stories, and beneficiaries.
- **AI**: artificial intelligence that helps analyze proof and summarize impact.

Brand meaning:

**Aidnara AI means human-centered aid with AI-verified impact.**

## Official Tagline

**Transparent Aid, Verified Impact**

## Alternative Taglines

- **Real Aid, Verified Impact**
- **Trace Every Gift, Verify Every Impact**
- **Proof-Backed Giving on BNB Smart Chain**

## One Sentence Pitch

Aidnara AI is a transparent donation platform on BNB Smart Chain that helps donors track fund usage, view AI-generated impact reports, and verify certificate of impact records on-chain.

## Naming Criteria

- Coined or uncommon word.
- Easy to pronounce in Indonesian and English.
- Fits donation, proof, impact, AI, and blockchain.
- Short enough for domain, logo, and pitch deck.
- Must be checked manually for trademark, domain, GitHub, app store, and social handles before final use.

## Previous Shortlist

- **Aidnara AI**: aid + nara, sounds human and original.
- **Proofora**: proof + aura, good for verified impact.
- **DonaVanta**: donation + advantage, strong product feel.
- **Impactra AI**: impact + mantra/trace, clear for impact reporting.
- **Aivara Aid**: AI + vara, premium and uncommon.
- **Trustaara**: trust + antara/aura, local-global feel.
- **Benevia AI**: benefit + via, friendly and social.
- **Donatria**: donation + patria, suitable for public/social good.
- **Aidqira**: aid + kira, short and distinctive.
- **Proofluma**: proof + lumen, suggests clarity.

## Recommended Pick

**Aidnara AI**

Reason:

- More distinctive than generic verify-aid naming.
- Still implies aid and human impact.
- Works with blockchain and AI without sounding too generic.

## Required Availability Checks

- Trademark database.
- `.com`, `.id`, and `.ai` domains.
- GitHub organization/repository name.
- Instagram, X, TikTok, and LinkedIn handles.
- Google search exact match.
- Product Hunt and app store search.
