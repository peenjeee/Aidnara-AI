# Aidnara AI Logo Brief For Google Stitch

## Brand

Name: **Aidnara AI**

Tagline: **Transparent Aid, Verified Impact**

Meaning: **human-centered aid with AI-verified impact**.

Aidnara AI combines transparent donation, BNB Smart Chain verification, AI proof analysis, and impact certificates.

## Logo Goal

Create a modern, trustworthy, and human-centered logo for a Web3 donation platform. The logo should feel transparent, secure, social-impact driven, and AI-enabled without looking too crypto-heavy.

## Visual Keywords

- transparent
- verified
- trustworthy
- human aid
- impact
- blockchain
- AI analysis
- certificate
- donation
- clarity

## Symbol Direction

Use one simple symbol that combines these ideas:

- A helping hand or abstract human figure for aid.
- A shield, checkmark, or verified badge for trust.
- Subtle connected nodes or chain links for blockchain.
- A soft spark, lens, or circuit curve for AI.

Avoid making the logo too busy. One clear icon is better than many small symbols.

## Preferred Style

- Clean SaaS/Web3 identity.
- Rounded but professional.
- Minimal geometric icon.
- Works as app icon, website logo, and certificate watermark.
- No mascot.
- No complex 3D.
- No generic charity heart-only logo.
- No aggressive crypto coin style.

## Color Palette

Primary:

- Deep navy: `#0B1220`
- Trust blue: `#2563EB`
- Cyan glow: `#06B6D4`

Accent:

- Verified green: `#22C55E`
- BNB gold: `#F3BA2F`

Neutral:

- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Slate: `#64748B`

Usage note: use BNB gold as a subtle accent only, not the main color.

## Typography Direction

- Modern sans-serif.
- Rounded geometric feel.
- Clear uppercase/lowercase readability.
- Similar feel to Inter, Satoshi, Manrope, or Plus Jakarta Sans.
- Wordmark should read clearly as `Aidnara AI`.

## Logo Variants Needed

- Full logo: icon + `Aidnara AI` wordmark.
- Horizontal logo for website header.
- Icon-only version for favicon/app icon.
- Monochrome version.
- Dark background version.
- Light background version.
- Certificate watermark version.

## Main Google Stitch Prompt

```text
Create a modern logo for "Aidnara AI", a transparent donation and impact verification platform powered by BNB Smart Chain and AI.

The brand meaning is human-centered aid with AI-verified impact. Design a clean geometric icon that subtly combines human aid, verification, blockchain transparency, and AI analysis. Use an abstract helping hand or human shape, a checkmark or shield-like verified form, and subtle connected node/chain details. The logo should feel trustworthy, transparent, social-impact driven, and modern Web3 without looking like a generic crypto coin.

Use a deep navy, trust blue, cyan, verified green, and a small BNB gold accent. Keep the icon simple, scalable, and suitable for website header, app icon, certificate watermark, and pitch deck. Pair it with a clean modern sans-serif wordmark reading "Aidnara AI".

Style: minimal, professional, rounded geometric, SaaS/Web3, high trust, high clarity. Avoid mascots, complex 3D, excessive gradients, generic charity hearts, and aggressive crypto visuals.
```

## Short Prompt

```text
Logo for "Aidnara AI". Modern Web3 donation platform on BNB Smart Chain with AI-verified impact reports. Create a clean geometric icon combining human aid, verification checkmark, subtle blockchain nodes, and AI clarity. Use navy, blue, cyan, verified green, and small BNB gold accent. Minimal, trustworthy, scalable, professional SaaS/Web3 style. Wordmark: Aidnara AI. Tagline: Transparent Aid, Verified Impact.
```

## Icon-Only Prompt

```text
Create an icon-only logo for Aidnara AI, a transparent donation and AI impact verification platform. The icon should combine a helping hand or human-centered shape with a verification checkmark and subtle blockchain node pattern. Use navy, blue, cyan, green, and a small BNB gold accent. Minimal, rounded, scalable, trustworthy, suitable for favicon and app icon.
```

## Certificate Watermark Prompt

```text
Create a subtle monochrome watermark version of the Aidnara AI logo for impact certificates. Use a minimal verified-aid symbol with a soft blockchain node detail. It should be elegant, low contrast, professional, and readable behind certificate text without distracting from the content.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
Avoid cartoon mascot, charity heart-only logo, medical cross, overly complex blockchain network, coin logo, 3D render, heavy gradients, unreadable typography, generic AI robot, lock-only cybersecurity logo, and overly futuristic neon style.
```

## Selection Criteria

- Readable at small size.
- Icon still recognizable without wordmark.
- Looks credible for donation and certificates.
- Has subtle Web3/BNB identity without copying BNB logo.
- Feels human and trustworthy, not speculative or hype-driven.
- Works on both dark and light backgrounds.
